Travel Packing Checklist

  1. Clothing: Pack versatile clothing that can be layered for different weather conditions.
  2. Toiletries: Bring travel-sized toiletries and personal care items.
  3. Documents: Ensure you have your passport, tickets, and any necessary travel documents.
  4. Electronics: Pack chargers, adapters, and any necessary electronic devices.
  5. First Aid Kit: Include basic medical supplies such as bandages, pain relievers, and any prescription medications.
